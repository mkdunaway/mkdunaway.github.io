Step Two

REMEMBER!!! Before you press cut, you need to mirror your design. You can do this is "Sure Cuts Alot".

For example five different materials could be: 1/8 birch ply, 1/16 birch ply, Amazon box, copy paper, 1/8 acrylic. Or it could be: nori, your jeans, bamboo ply, etc. Basically, you can play it safe or get creative. Whatever you choose to cut, make sure it's laser safe! Use this guide among many others to determine that. Remember, YOU bare the burden of proof when you want to cut something you're bringing in. Do your research.
