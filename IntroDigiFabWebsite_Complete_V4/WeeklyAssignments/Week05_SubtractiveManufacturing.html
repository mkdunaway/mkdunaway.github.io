Assignment: Learn how to use the Axiom CNC router...and be creative.
Due Wednesday, Sept 30th by Midnight

There are a few things I'd like to point out about this assignment:

  1. Receiving the first Axiom training does not grant you full access to use the Axiom. Rather, it means you can begin to imagine different projects you can create using the Axiom. If you'd like to use it again, you will need to loop me in.

  2. This is the first assignment where one component of your grade will be based on creativity. To some, this may seem subjective so I have linked a rubric here for your reference.

  3. If you're curious about what type of work would recieve a 100% for this week, check out Josep's page.
Step One

Get trained on the Axiom. You MUST watch this video before your training.

To document your training, I suggest a few things:

  1. Go into the shop and take a few photos of important tools or buttons

  2. Sit down in the lab on the computer with the software VCarve and take a few photos

  3. Youtube it. Take screenshots of other people using CNCs
Step Two

Like I mentioned, this is the first week where you will recieve a grade for creativity. You have been building up muscle around tools, machines, and software and now we're switching gears a bit. I want you to imagine you have a 2' by 4' piece of 3/4 inch plywood. The nice kind. Using a software of your choice, create a design to hypothetically create. You need to include:

  1. A 2D drawing of how the piece of wood will be cut. You could use Illustrator or Fusion 360 for this... or another software.

  2. Create a computer render or hand drawing of what it will look like when done

  3. Include inspiration and research.

  4. Assume once you cut the design, you will be doing some post process work. Include a bill of materials. Will you need wood stain? Maybe you need electronics? What will you need to finish this project to your satisfaction?
