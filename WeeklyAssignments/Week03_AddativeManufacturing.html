Assignment: 3D print a pot.
Due Monday by Midnight

PRO TIP: 3D printing takes a long time. Your pot could take between two and eight hours to print. As soon as your pot is ready, reserve a printer.

Step One

Understand the assignment requirements:

  • Your pot must fit inside the circle cut you made on your wooden CNC routed desk organizer.

  • You must document using text a screenshots that you implemented offset (in sketch mode), loft, offset planes, pattern, and shell.

Step Two

If you feel like you still aren't following along well with Fusion, I advise going back to some beginner tutorials. The video below was created a year ago by our last TA Kevin Elkhoury ('21). He became a Fusion expert and I highly recommend this tutorial. In my video, I will move quickly and will not explain foundational elements you should have picked up from last week.

Step Three

Time to design your pot. DO NOT make a pot exactly like mine! Be creative! The tools we will use can be utilized in a variety of ways!
