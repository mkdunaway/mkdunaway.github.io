Assignment: Make a website.
Due Wednesday, Sept 2nd by Midnight

The purpose of your first assignment is to expose you to basic web design workflows. Building a website is a useful skill and effetively supports a variety of documentation for your work. A few key things you will be introduced to include GitHub, HTML, and Brackets. This will be very difficult if you've never done it before so try to be patient. Go through the videos we've created and linked. Alot of the direction may sound like nonsense and that's ok!

Examples of websites similar to the one you will make can be found here. A few that stand out include Leon's, Josep's, and Aleza's. There are hundereds of great examples. It will be important to have a way to navigate to all weekly assignments. This could be achieved through a navigation bar or a sidebar of sorts. Other than simple navgation, the website is yours to make your own!
