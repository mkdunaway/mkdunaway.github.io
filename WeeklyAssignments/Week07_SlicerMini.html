Assignment: Use the laser cutter to make a 3D cardboard object.
Due Wednesday, Oct 14th by Midnight

The next two weeks will be one larger more complex project. We will create a small iteration of the project this week so you get a handle on the workflow. Eventually, this project will need to be big-ish. To bring some objectivity to that statement, it will need to be at least 25" on one axis. You will need to prove that by holding up a measuring tape to it. It also must have at least 12 "slices". No more than 6 sheets of cardboard. However, this week we will create a tiny version that's only 5 or so inches on it's longesrt axis.
